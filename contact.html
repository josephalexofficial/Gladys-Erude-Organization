Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Check out our most frequently asked questions below or contact us directly.

How can I participate in the Memorial Run?

The GEM Run is free to participate! You can register online through our Google Form or register on the day of the event at Tigoi Primary School. The event includes a 5K run/walk and free medical screenings.

What payment methods do you accept for donations?

We accept donations through GoFundMe, M-PESA Paybill (522522), and Cash App. All donations go directly to supporting Tigoi Primary School infrastructure improvements and our community programs.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, The Gladys Erude Organization is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the USA. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How can I get involved as a partner?

We welcome community partners, corporate sponsors, and government collaborators. Please contact us through our contact form or email info@gladyserudeorganization.org for partnership opportunities.
