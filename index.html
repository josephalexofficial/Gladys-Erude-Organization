Welcome to The Gladys Erude Organization

The Gladys Erude Organization is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with official status in both the United States and Kenya, dedicated to empowering women and children in underserved communities in Kenya and providing essential services.

Our Commitment

Empowering Communities Building Futures

By 2030, GEO aims to empower at least 10,000 women and children in underserved communities across Kenya by providing access to essential services such as education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
