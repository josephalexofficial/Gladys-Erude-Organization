Featured Story

All Roads Lead to Tigoi for the Annual Gladys Erude Organization Memorial Run

Vihiga County, Kenya – All roads across Western Kenya — from Vihiga, Trans Nzoia, Kakamega, Kisumu, to Nandi — will converge at Tigoi Primary School on 6th September 2025 at 7:00 AM for the highly anticipated Gladys Erude Organization Memorial Run. Now in its annual edition, the event is more than just a race. It is a powerful community initiative aimed at transforming lives and uplifting the vulnerable.

This year, the proceeds will go towards the renovation of Tigoi Primary School, providing sanitary towels to young girls from disadvantaged backgrounds, and supporting local women through empowerment programs.
